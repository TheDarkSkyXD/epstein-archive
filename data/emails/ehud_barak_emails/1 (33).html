Join Origins Project director Lawrence Krauss for an exclusive 10-day cruise to Antarctica aboard the luxurious Silver Explorer, December 2-12, 2015.

 

Experience Antarctica with the Origins Project’s expedition cruise and discover a frosted wilderness of glistening white. Our Antarctic cruise will transport you to the wonders of this surreal landscape. Marvel at calving glaciers. See iceberg sculptures like enormous works of avant-garde art. Visit historic whaling sites. From seeing seals sunbathing on slow-moving ice floes to hearing the calls of penguins by the thousands, astounding animal encounters found nowhere else on earth are yours to experience with the Origins Project.

 

Krauss, also Foundation Professor in Physics and the School of Earth and Space Exploration at Arizona State University, will lead you on trips throughout space and time as our ship explores the beauty of this southern continent. Topics covered will include science at the South Pole, what we are learning with today’s biggest telescopes, and what the Antarctic us telling us about climate change.
